FRIED CHICKEN

ingredients

  • 2 whole free range,organic chickens
  • vegetable oil of choice for frying the chicken
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 5 tablespoons salt
  • 4 tablespoons ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons cavenne pepper
  • 2 cups of buttermilk

how to prepare it

  1. dip chicken in eggs 😏
  2. place in flour mixture 😀
  3. a few pieces at time 🍗
  4. turn to coat 😃
  6. in a deepfat fryer heat oil 375 🍲
  7. working in batches fry chicken several pieces at a time until golden brown
